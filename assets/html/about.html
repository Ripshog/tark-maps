My Journey Into Tarkov

          Greetings fellow PMCs. Let me start off by saying, I am rather new to this game. I have been trying to come up with a way for me to better my understanding of Escape from Tarkov, and be more of a contributing member to my squad, and feel less like I'm being carried through raids. To that end, I have put together this site to help me with learning the maps so that I do not struggle as much with positioning.

If you feel you are in the same boat as I am, or if you are an experienced player who just wants a leg up by having an easily availiable set of maps, this is the site for you.

I cannot emphasize enough that I DID NOT create these maps. I simply added labels on the Customs and Shoreline maps, and created this site to make them easy to display. All credit for each map goes to the map makers. The Customs and Shoreline maps are availiable at:
